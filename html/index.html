Admission Process

Our admission process is simple and student-friendly. Follow these easy steps to apply:

  • Fill out the online application form
  • Submit required documents (transcripts, ID, etc.)
  • Attend the interview or entrance exam
  • Receive admission confirmation
Important Dates

Mark your calendar with these key dates:

  • Application Start Date: June 1, 2024
  • Application Deadline: August 15, 2024
  • Entrance Exam: August 25, 2024
  • Results Announcement: September 1, 2024
Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, ensure you meet our eligibility requirements:

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5
  • Proficiency in English (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Relevant documents for specialized courses
